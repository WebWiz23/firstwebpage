The first Web page

In the beginning,Tim created HyperText Markup Language.
The internet was without form and void,and text was upon the face
of the monitor and the hands of of Tim were moving over the
face of the keyboard. And Tim said,
let there be links;and there were links.
And Tim saw that the links were good. And Tim separated the links from the text.
Tim called the links Anchors,and the text He called Other Stuffs. And the whole thing put
together was the first web page.
